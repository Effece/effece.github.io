Welcome to my Rubik's Cube page!

Here, I store various sorts of data about my Rubik's Cubes.

Website exploration

Every page is accessible through the yellow menu on the left. You can develop it by clicking on it.
I store all the times and sort the best ones here. You can submit one time at a time or a full session at once.
Algorithms are mostly for the CFOP method, divised between OLL algorithms and PLL algorithms. You can add an algorithm by filling this form. If you attempt to modify an existing algorithm, you will be given the opportunity to overwrite some of its attributes. You can also change attributes via the modify an algorithm page. The evaluation criterias are explained on the related page.
The collection page displays all my puzzles.Upcoming features are listed on their page. You can record a practice session for every algorithm with the recording page.
I've listed a bunch of useful websites at the bottom of the yellow panel.
