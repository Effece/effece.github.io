Welcome to my Rubik's Cube page!

Here, I store various sorts of data about my Rubik's Cubes.

Website exploration

Every page is accessible through the yellow menu on the left. You can develop it by clicking on it.
Algorithms are mostly for the CFOP method, divised between OLL algorithms and PLL algorithms. You can add an algorithm by filling this form. If you attempt to modify an existing algorithm, you will be given the opportunity to overwrite some of its attributes. You can also change attributes via the modify an algorithm page.
The evaluation criterias are explained on the related page. Upcoming features are listed on their page.
